Our Story

In 2023 Binance abruptly froze a European user’s account, trapping €30 000 in bitcoin. Support tickets, chats, and formal notices were stonewalled for months. Investigation revealed dozens of similar cases worldwide. Binance Victim was born to fight back with facts, lawyers, regulators, and public scrutiny.
